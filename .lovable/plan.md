
# Azdine Éco - Landing Page de Formation en Comptabilité

## Overview
Landing page à forte conversion pour les formations en comptabilité pratique du professeur Azdine Éco, ciblant le marché marocain avec contenu bilingue (Français + Darija) et conversion via WhatsApp.

## Brand Colors (from logo)
- Primary Gold/Yellow: `#F5B731` → `#E5A100` gradient
- Dark/Black: `#1A1A1A`
- Accent Gold: `#FFC107`
- White backgrounds with gold/black accents

## Logo
The uploaded logo (Logo_Azdine_Eco.jpg) will be copied into the project assets and used in the header and footer.

## Page Structure (single index route)

### 1. **Header** — Sticky navigation with logo, nav links, and WhatsApp CTA button

### 2. **Hero Section** — Bold headline in French + Darija subtext, strong CTA "S'inscrire maintenant" linking to WhatsApp with pre-filled message, trust badges (attestation, pratique, marché marocain)

### 3. **Problème / Solution** — Copywriting section: "Vous cherchez une formation pratique en comptabilité?" → solution positioning with icons

### 4. **Présentation du Formateur** — Prof. Azdine Éco bio section with credibility markers

### 5. **Packs de Formation** — Two pricing cards side by side:
   - Pack 1: 3 mois (bases + pratique)
   - Pack 2: 6 mois (formation complète) — highlighted as "Recommandé"
   - Each with "Choisir ce pack" WhatsApp CTA

### 6. **Certification** — Attestation de formation + Certificat de réussite visual section

### 7. **Section Darija (RTL)** — Arabic/Darija content block for local trust and accessibility

### 8. **Vidéos YouTube** — Embedded YouTube videos from the channel

### 9. **Réseaux Sociaux** — Links to Instagram, Facebook, YouTube with icons

### 10. **CTA Final** — Large banner with "Commencez votre carrière en comptabilité" + WhatsApp button

### 11. **Footer** — Contact info, social links, WhatsApp, copyright

## Key Features
- All CTA buttons link to `https://wa.me/212701117636` with pre-filled messages

- Mobile-first responsive design
- Smooth scroll navigation
- Bilingual content (French primary, Darija sections in RTL)
